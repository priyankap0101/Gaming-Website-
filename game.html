Game Reviews

Skate Series

Developed by EA Black Box, the Skate series offers a more realistic skateboarding experience with its innovative "flick-it" control scheme. Players can perform tricks using the right analog stick, creating a more immersive gameplay experience.

Session

A skateboarding simulation game that focuses on realism and authenticity. Players have precise control over their skateboard, allowing them to perform realistic tricks and create custom skate spots.

Skate or Die Series

An old-school series that includes games like Skate or Die and Skate or Die 2: The Search for Double Trouble. These games offer a mix of skateboarding and mini-games, providing a nostalgic experience for players.

Tony Hawk's Pro Skater Series

This iconic series features various installments, including Tony Hawk's Pro Skater, Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 2, and so on. Players can perform tricks, complete objectives, and explore skateboarding environments.
